- Press any sort button to do so by its own category.

- Pressing will also toggle whether the next press will be ascending or descending.

- Ascending () starts from lowest to highest value.

- Descending () starts from highest to lowest value.

- Press a filter button to toggle its state. Changes are applied immediately.

- Double-click a filter to set others in the same category to the same state.

- Double-clicking an OFF filter will enable all, since its state after the first click was ON, and vice versa.

- Click on a ship's level to change it.

- You may set the level of all ships in a category through Options -> Mass-Level.

- Click on a category's name cell to open the editor.

- Click on a ship's level to change it.

- Click on a category's level header (Lv.) to change all ship levels in said category.
